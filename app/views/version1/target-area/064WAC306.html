Flood alert for Coast from Fairlight to Dungeness including the Tidal Rother

Get flood warnings by phone, text or email

Updated {{ "now" | govukTime }} on {{ "today" | govukDate }}

High tides, strong winds and large waves this morning may lead to flooding.

Flooding is possible from 10:45am to 2:45pm on 23 October 2025.

Low lying land near the coast is expected to be most affected, particularly around Rye Harbour, Camber and Jury's Gap. The detailed forecast is for a peak tide of 4.11m AoD at Rye at 12:45pm BST, with Force 8 north westerly winds and 2.4m waves. This level includes a surge of 0.35m.

We are closely monitoring the situation and closing flood gates.

Avoid beaches and coastal promenades, which may be flooded.

This message will be updated by 9:00PM on 23 October 2025 or as the situation changes.

View map of the flood alert area(Visual only)

Flood alert area: Coast and tidal areas from Fairlight to Dungeness including Dungeness, Lydd, Camber, Winchelsea Beach, Pett Level and the Tidal Rother to Rye, Rye Harbour and East Guldeford.
