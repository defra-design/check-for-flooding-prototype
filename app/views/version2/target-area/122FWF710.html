Flood warning for River Ouse at York - riverside properties

Updated {{ "now" | govukTime }} on {{ "today" | govukDate }}

River levels are falling but remain high today, Monday 17 November 2025, and flooding is still expected. There is no further significant rainfall expected and levels will continue to fall slowly in the coming days. Locations most affected will be properties on King's Staith in York and riverside areas through York from Lendall Bridge to Millennium Bridge. We are closely monitoring the situation. The Foss Barrier is in operation. Take care and avoid walking, cycling or driving through flood water. Act on your flood plan if you have one. This message will be updated by 3:30PM on 17th November 2025 or as the situation changes.
